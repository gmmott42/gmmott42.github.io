Western Hemisphere

Looking at our side of Earth



When beginning our exploration around the Globe we start with something familiar to us, the United States. In this section, we will be looking at the regions of the United States and how those impact us. Then we will move into Canada and how it is broken up. Next, we begin to branch out and look at our neighbors to the south of Central and South America. These are very distinct geographic regions that share a rich culture and history. We will explore these and see how various historical events shaped who they are today. This information will focus our learning on the beginning ideas of how history and geography shape what we know as culture.
