About Me

Hello, my name is Mr. Mott and I am going to be your guide through geography. I am beginning my sixth year in education and this year I am teaching 5th graders. I have taught Kindergarten through 5th grade, Special Education, and 7th grade World Geography.
When thinking about the content of this course a quote from Robert Burdette (an American humorist) comes to mind,

“Don’t believe the World owes you a living. The World owes you nothing, it was here first”.
This quote is not to discourage you or dissuade you from achieving your dreams, rather it is to simply remind you your dreams are yours for the taking!

I have a degree from Missouri State University in Elementary Education with an emphasis in Middle School Social Studies and English. I am in the Online Education program at the University of Missouri and am currently in my final semester expected to graduate in May of 2022
